Sigil of Harmony
'You can feel 2 forces trying to stay in balance as you hold this'
Activates Shattered Balance mode
While Shattered Balance mode is on bosses are able to be summoned any time despite requirements
The bosses can be possessed by Yin or Yang based on time of day
Yin makes the bosses stronger and faster but less durable
Yang makes the bosses weaker and slower but more durable
Killing a possessed boss gives a Shattered Balance boss item

Obtained at a Ruined Shrine on a wall

Change amulets to weapons potentially at the end game?
